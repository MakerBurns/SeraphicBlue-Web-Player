Extend

This is where you "extend" your items, that is, upgrade your items.


Definition of Extend

Items that you can use in battle can be purchased from shops.
Extend can enhance items' performance and potency.
Extended items are very useful especially when fighting against strong enemies, whereby original usable items are just not strong enough to support the party members.
Therefore extended items are essential in winning many battles.


Necessary items to perform Extend

These are the 2 types of items needed in order to perform Extend:

Source Item The original usable item that you want to Extend.
ÇdÇw Material The catalyst needed together with the Source Items to perform Extend.


Types of Extended Items

The following are the 2 main types of Extended items.

All An extended item that works on all party members as opposed to the original items that works only on one party member.
Requires 4 of the original usable items and an EX Material to produce an All-effect usable item.
The new All-effect item will be renamed to A-(name of item), eg. Heal Bottle will be renamed to A-Heal Bottle.
Double An extended item that has double the healing effect (HP & TP) of its original item. As such, only double healing extended items are available.
Requires 2 of the original usable items and an EX Material to produce a Double-effect usable item.
The new Double-effect item will be renamed to D-(name of item), eg. Clana will be renamed to D-Clana.


Extend Level

There are 4 levels of Extend, and each level corresponds to which type of usable items that you can extend.
So it's important to check which items correspond to which Ex Level first via the Extend menu before buying the appropriate Ex Materials.

Related Extend Information

As explained above, different Source Items correspond to different Extend levels, depending on the strengths of the Source items.
