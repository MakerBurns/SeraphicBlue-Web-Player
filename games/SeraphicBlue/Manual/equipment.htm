Equipment

Character's Equipment List

The table below briefly explains the 3 main types of equipments that a character can equip in Seraphic Blue:

Weapon An equipment that a character holds and is used for attacking purposes. Press Enter/Space to equip a new Weapon.
Frame An equipment that a character wears on his/her body to protect himself/herself. Press Enter/Space to equip a new Frame.
Sphere An accessory that boosts characters' attributes and abilities. Press Enter/Space on a Sphere twice to equip a new Sphere. Press once to switch slots with another Sphere.

Pressing Shift unequips an equipment.
Please be aware that unequipping a new Frame or equipping a new Frame with lesser Sphere slots than the total number of Spheres equipped will unequip all the Spheres as well.

(More info on Frames and Spheres can be found in the Frame/Sphere page.)

Equipment Selection List

This is where players can select a new equipment to equip a character. The top of the picture shows the respective equipment description, and the left of the picture shows the list of equipments.

The right of the picture shows how a character's stats are affected when the cursor highlights a certain equipment.
Red arrow indicates a stat increase while blue arrow indicates a stat decrease. (More on the stats' abbrieviations can be found here.)

SPC refers to Sphere Capacity, which indicates the total number of Sphere slots that a character can equip Spheres in. Frames are the ones that contain Sphere slots, so if a character isn't equipped with a Frame, he/she cannot equip spheres. Different Frames contain different number of Sphere slots.
