Page ÇQÅ^Stats

ATK (Attack) Defines a character's physical damage output (Applies to physical abilities as well).
HIT (Hit) Defines a character's attack hit rate (Applicable to Normal & Hard Attack).
CRT (Critical) Defines a character's critical hit rate (Only applicable to Normal Attack).
ATKWT (Attack Waiting Time) Defines how much time a character has to wait for his/her next turn after a Normal Attack.
DEF (Defense) Defines a character's physical damage resistance (Applies to physical abilities as well).
AVD (Avoidance) Defines a character's evasion rate against normal physical attacks.
MEN (Mentality) Defines a character's mental (magical) damage output (Applies to magical abilities as well).
MDF (Mental Defense) Defines a character's mental (magical) damage resistance (Applies to magical abilities as well).
AGL (Agility) Defines the amount of waiting time (WT) for a character's next turn. Higher agility means lesser WT.
DEX (Dexterity) Defines a battle's starting WT positions, rate of pre-emptive and surprise attacks (based on all allies' DEX VS all enemies' DEX), escape rate (based on an ally's DEX VS all enemies' DEX), and rate of Counter Attack/Advanced Guard in reaction to enemies normal' physical attacks (usually on a one-to-one basis).
SPC (Sphere Capacity) Defines the number of Sphere slots for equipping Spheres in a character's Frame.

Å¶AGL & DEXÅ^Rate of Speed & Rate of Reaction
Agility (AGI) is one's physical movement speed while Dexterity (DEX) is one's skill in physical movement.
In most RPGs, agility and dexterity are key factors in winning battles.
Advantages of agility and dexterity include "the one who is faster will have more actions over his/her enemies" and "the one with greater dexterity will be able to perform more first strikes and have higher chances of performing certain actions successfully ".
