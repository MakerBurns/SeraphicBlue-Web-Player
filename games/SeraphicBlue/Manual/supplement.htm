Supplement

The supplement is an alternative to HP and TP healing items. Players can fill active party members' HP & TP from the supplement in this menu.


How to Navigate in Supplement Menu

Below explains how to navigate in Supplement menu:

As one can see in the Supply section, HP has 4 digits while TP has 3 digits, so the max that one can fill for HP is 9999 and for TP is 999.
But how high you can fill up your character's HP/TP depends on the Supplement status and your character's HP/TP (eg. If you can fill about 3000 HP to a character but the Supplement status shows that it only has 2000 HP left, you can only fill up to max of 2000 HP).


Different Sections Explanation

Below explains the different sections of the Supplement menu:

Current Tells you your character's current HP/TP (will change when you adjust the number digits in the Supply Section)
Deficiency The difference between your character's total and current HP/TP
Percentage This tells you the percentage of your character's current HP/TP
Supply This is where you fill your character's HP/TP
Auto Fill Auto fill your characters' HP/TP
Supplement Status Supplement's status (Lvl, HP, TP, and Next)


Supplement Level Up Å^SUP.EXP

Just like characters, Supplement also has its own levelling system (as depicted in the picture above).
Supplement levels up by gaining supplement experience (SUP.EXP) which can be earned by defeating enemies.
As Supplement gains higher levels, it also gains more HP and TP.

Below explains the terms used in the Supplement Status:

SUP.LV Supplement Level
SUP.HP Supplement HP
SUP.TP Supplement TP
NEXT The amount of SUP.EXP needed to level up


Auto Fill


Auto Fill allows all characters' HP and TP to automatically be fully filled (by means of Supplement) after a battle.
An "Auto Fill Announcement" will appear on the map screen after a battle (as shown above), indicating how much HP/TP has been supplied to the characters and how much Supplement's HP/TP remains.
(There are times however when the Auto Fill Announce doesn't appear due to a cutscene happening right after a battle. Do not be alarmed, as in this case, the Auto Fill will still occur, just that you won't get to see the Auto-Fill Announcement.)
However do be aware that if Supplement's HP/TP drops to 0, Auto-fill won't work anymore and characters won't be able to recover HP/TP via Supplement as a result, so always be on the lookout on the remaining Supplement HP/TP.

Players can turn on/off HP/TP Auto Fill by pressing Shift while in the Supplement menu.

Supplement Charge

Supplement is limited and can run out.
Hence it is necessary to refill Supplement, and below are the two ways to do so.

Rest (Inns) Players can fully restore Supplement and characters' HP & TP by resting in inns.
Resurrect Point Resurrect Points are blue sphere objects that are usually located next to Memory Trees.
To make use of Resurrect Points to recover characters' and Supplement's HP/TP, players have to buy Resurrect Spheres in shops (found under Miscellaneous section). There are 4 levels of Resurrect Spheres which indicate different strengths of HP/TP recovery.
