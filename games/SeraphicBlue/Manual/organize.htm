Forward & Back

Characters in the Forward category are the active party members, while those in the Back category are the reserve party members.
Up to a max of 4 characters can be in the active party at any one time.
If there are more than 4 characters, the extra characters will be moved to the Back category.

By the way, for any EXP gained by the Forward party members by defeating an enemy, the Back party members will also gain the same EXP.
This is so that the Back party members do not fall behind in level from the Forward party members, and players do not have to worry about having to switch characters all the time to balance the characters' levels.
