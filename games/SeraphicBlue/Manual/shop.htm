Players can buy items at all item shops or from shopkeepers (when players don't have access to shops).

Buy Menu

◆How to Navigate Buy Menu

The left of the menu is the list of item categories that a player can select.
（Selecting the Sphere category will bring up 4 Sphere Categories to select）
↓
The right of the menu is the item list that appears when a player selects an item category and also shows the cost of the respective items. (for 1 item).
↓
Once you have selected an item you want to buy,
・Pressing left or right will increase or decrease quantity of items you want to buy by 1.
・Pressing up or down will increase or decrease quantity of items you want to buy by 10.
↓
Pressing Enter/Space will confirm the purchase; pressing Esc will cancel it.

◆Character View when Selecting Equipments
When buying equipments, players can check if a certain character can equip a certain equipment by looking at the character sprite at the bottom left of the Buy menu. A walking animation will indicate that the character is able to equip a highlighted equipment.
Players can switch between characters by pressing Shift. (Don't press Shift too many times to avoid the StickKeys stiuation.)

Highlighting a weapon or a frame will display stat changes to a characterif the weapon or frame is to be equipped to the character.
If a stat acronym (ATK, HIT etc) turns red, it is an increase in that particular stat. The opposite is true if the stat acronym turns dark blue.
Spheres with stat boosts, however, do not display any stat changes if you highlight them.
This is because all the spheres with stat boosts specifically state which stats are boosted in their descriptions.
To see the exact stat changes for a new equipment, players can highlight the new equipment while equipping characters in the Equipment menu.

◆Discount System
Prices of items are discounted if players buy 10 or more of the same items.
Higher discounts are given for more of the same items bought, so it would be more economical to buy more of the same items at the same time than buy the same number of items on separate occasions.
The discount system, however, does not apply to equipments.
The discount system is especially very useful for eg. Extending where players can buy a large amount of Source items for high discounts to produce a high amount of All/Double-effect items, which is cheaper than buying the same amount on separate occasions.

◆Item Lineup／Game Progression Changes
New items may be updated to the shops' items lineup if players sell exceedingly large amounts of items and visit the shops on another occasion.
All shops in the world of Seraphic Blue sell the same universal items, so all shops will be updated with the exact same items.
The shops' items lineups are also updated to the next level as the game progresses (New places to visit, new storyline development etc).
Hence be sure to check the shops on a regular basis for any new updates.

Sell Menu

◆How to navigate Sell Menu

The left of the menu is the list of item categories that a player can select.
（Selecting the Sphere category will bring up 4 Sphere Categories to select）
↓
The right of the menu is the item list that appears when a player selects an item category and also shows the money that you will get back by selling the respective items (for 1 item).
↓
Once you have selected an item you want to sell,
・Pressing left or right will increase or decrease quantity of items you want to buy by 1.
・Pressing up or down will increase or decrease quantity of items you want to buy by 10.
↓
Pressing Enter/Space will confirm the purchase; pressing Esc will cancel it.
