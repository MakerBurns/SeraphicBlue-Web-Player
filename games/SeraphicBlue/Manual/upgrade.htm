Upgrade

This is where you upgrade your consumables to stronger ones.


Definition of Upgrade

Items that you can use in battle can be purchased from shops.
Upgrading can enhance items' performance and potency.
Upgraded items are especially very useful when fighting against strong enemies, whereby original consumables are just not strong enough to support the party members.
Therefore upgraded items are essential in winning many battles.


Necessary items to perform Upgrade

These are the 2 types of items needed in order to perform Extend:

Source Item The original consumable item that you want to Upgrade.
Up Substance   The catalyst needed to perform upgrade on Source Items.


Types of Extended Items

The following are the 2 main types of upgraded items.

All An upgraded item that works on all party members as opposed to the original items that works only on one party member.
Requires 4 of the original usable items and an EX Material to produce an All-effect consumable.
The new All-effect item will be renamed to A-(name of item), eg. Heal Bottle will be renamed to A-Heal Bottle.
Double An upgraded item that has double the healing effect (HP & TP) of its original item. As such, only double healing upgraded items exist.
Requires 2 of the original usable items and an EX Material to produce a Double-effect consumable..
The new Double-effect item will be renamed to D-(name of item), eg. Clana will be renamed to D-Clana.


Upgrade Level

There are 4 levels of Upgrade, and each level corresponds to which type of consumables that you can upgrade.
So it's important to check which items correspond to which Upgrade Level first via the Extend menu before buying the appropriate Up Substances.

Items with Upgrade descriptions

As explained above, different Source Items correspond to different Extend levels, depending on the strengths of the Source items.
