Dictionary

This is where all information on Seraphic Blue is stored in. Players can learn more about the history and storyline of Seraphic Blue here.
The above picture shows the 5 sections of the Dictionary that players can access to.


Character Data

Players can learn about character profiles here. This section gets updated as the game progresses and players learn of new characters.


Glossary

Players can learn about Seraphic Blue's terminologies and places of interest here. This section gets updated as the game progresses and players learn of new terms and places.


Story Review

Players can read past episode summaries after completing past episodes in here.
Move up and down to scroll through episodes, and left and right to scroll through chapters.


History

This section displays the history of Seraphic Blue in chronological form.
At the start of the game, this section is almost empty, but as the story progresses and players learn of something new in reagrds to the history of Seraphic Blue, this section will gradually be updated.


Destination

This section gives players information on their next destination, and their objective.
