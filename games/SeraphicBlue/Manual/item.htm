Item

This is the Item menu where you can see items that you currently posess and also use items.


How to Navigate Item Menu

Below explains how to navigate the Item menu.

The left of the menu is the list of item categories that a player can select.
（Selecting the Sphere category will bring up 4 Sphere Categories to select）
↓
The right of the menu is the item list that appears when a player selects an item category and also shows the respective items' quantity.
↓
（※Relating to Usable Items）
A player can select an item from the item listto use on a character.


Item Categories

There are 7 Item Categories as listed below:

Consumables  Consumable items such as HP & TP healing items, Negative Status effect removal items etc.
These items can also be used in battle in addition to in the Item menu.
Weapon Weapon equipment.
Frame Frame equipment.
Sphere Sphere equipment.
Miscellaneous Items that are not any of the above-mentioned items fall under this category.
They mainly consist of Resurrection Spheres, EX Materials and sellable Loots.
Key Items Items that are either of important relevance to the storyline or are required to progress through dungeons (keys etc.) are found in this category.
Newly Acquired The 8 newly-acquired items that players buy from shops or obtain in-game are listed here.
However, if either a new usable item's quantity is exhausted or a new equipment is equipped to a character, those items will no longer display in this category and will appear as blank spaces.

※Sellable Loots
Literally, sellable loots are items that can be sold to shops for extra money. They have no other uses whatsoever.
Loots can be obtained at a certain drop rate by defeating enemies. They are not just limited to sellable loots, but also rare equipments not found in shops.
