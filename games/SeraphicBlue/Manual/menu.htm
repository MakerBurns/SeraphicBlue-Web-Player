Pressing Esc while on the map will bring you to the main menu as shown above.
The main menu displays information like character stats, amount of Dear (money), no. of Encounters, playtime, current episode name and current location.
The console displays 9 selectaable options.

ITEM Brings you to the Item menu.
ABILITY Brings you to the Ability menu.
STATUS Brings you to the Status menu.
EQUIPMENT Brings you to the Equipment menu.
SUPPLEMENT Brings you to the Supplement menu.
DICTIONARY Brings you to the Encyclopedia menu.
ORGANIZE Brings you to the Organize menu.
EXTEND Brings you to the Extend menu.
BREAK Take a break from the game. This is useful if you want to stop playtime from running and also if you wanna sit back and listen to the BGM while you take a break. The playtime will not run at all even if you take a very long break. To go back to the game, just hit Enter/Space or Esc.
